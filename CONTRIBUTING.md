# Contributing to Nolus-Protocol

Thanks for your interest. A few things to know before you start.

## Who can contribute

Code contributions to most Nolus-Protocol repositories are limited to organization members and explicit collaborators. External pull requests and issues on these repos are blocked at the platform level.

If you'd like to contribute regularly or have a specific change in mind, reach us on our [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/nolus-protocol) to discuss becoming a collaborator.

## What not to do

- **Drive-by "fix typo" PRs, documentation tweaks, or trivial formatting changes are not accepted.** These patterns are typically airdrop-farming attempts.
- **Do not open issues or PRs generated by LLMs without human review.** We close these without triage. If you use AI tools, you are responsible for everything in the submission as if you wrote it yourself.
- Do not open public issues or PRs for security matters — see [SECURITY](./SECURITY.md).

## Code of Conduct

All contributors are expected to follow our [Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
